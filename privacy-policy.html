Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Nation Africa Clean Reader ("the Extension") is a browser extension that provides an ad-free, clean reading experience on nation.africa. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, cookies, or any other user information. The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

How It Works

The Extension runs locally in your browser on nation.africa pages only. It modifies the page display by removing advertisements and visual clutter using CSS and JavaScript injected into the page. No data leaves your browser as a result of the Extension's operation.

Permissions

The Extension requires access to nation.africa pages to inject its content scripts and stylesheets. It does not request access to any other websites or browser data.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
